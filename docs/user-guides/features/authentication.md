# Authentication Features

This guide covers all authentication-related features in the Kavach platform, including login, logout, password management, and security features.

## Logging In

### Standard Login Process

1. **Access the Login Page**
   - Click "Login" from the homepage
   - Or navigate directly to `/login`

2. **Enter Your Credentials**
   - Email address (the one you registered with)
   - Password (case-sensitive)

3. **Choose Account Type** (if applicable)
   - Customer login
   - Expert login
   - Admin login (for administrators only)

4. **Click "Sign In"**
   - You'll be redirected to your dashboard
   - Or to the page you were trying to access

### Remember Me Option

When logging in, you can check "Remember Me" to:
- Stay logged in for up to 30 days
- Avoid entering credentials on trusted devices
- Automatically refresh your session

**Security Note**: Only use "Remember Me" on personal, secure devices.

### Login Troubleshooting

#### Forgot Your Password?
1. Click "Forgot Password?" on the login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your email for reset instructions
4. Follow the link to create a new password

#### Account Locked?
If you see "Account temporarily locked":
- Wait 15 minutes before trying again
- This happens after 5 failed login attempts
- Contact support if the issue persists

#### Email Not Verified?
If you see "Please verify your email":
1. Check your email for the verification message
2. Click "Resend Verification" if needed
3. Contact support if you can't access your email

## Logging Out

### Standard Logout
- Click your profile picture/name in the top right
- Select "Logout" from the dropdown menu
- You'll be redirected to the homepage

### Automatic Logout
Your session will automatically expire:
- After 24 hours of inactivity (without "Remember Me")
- After 30 days (with "Remember Me")
- When you close your browser (depending on settings)

### Security Logout
If you suspect unauthorized access:
1. Log out immediately
2. Change your password
3. Review your account activity
4. Contact support if needed

## Password Management

### Changing Your Password

1. **Access Account Settings**
   - Go to your profile menu
   - Click "Account Settings"
   - Select "Security" tab

2. **Change Password Section**
   - Enter your current password
   - Enter your new password
   - Confirm your new password
   - Click "Update Password"

3. **Confirmation**
   - You'll receive an email confirmation
   - All other sessions will be logged out
   - You'll need to log in again

### Password Reset

If you've forgotten your password:

1. **Request Reset**
   - Go to the login page
   - Click "Forgot Password?"
   - Enter your email address

2. **Check Your Email**
   - Look for "Reset Your Kavach Password"
   - Click the reset link (valid for 1 hour)

3. **Create New Password**
   - Enter a new password meeting requirements
   - Confirm the new password
   - Click "Reset Password"

4. **Login with New Password**
   - Return to the login page
   - Use your new password to sign in

## Account Security Features

### Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

**What is 2FA?**
An extra layer of security requiring both your password and a verification code.

**Setting Up 2FA:**
1. Go to Account Settings > Security
2. Click "Enable Two-Factor Authentication"
3. Scan the QR code with your authenticator app
4. Enter the verification code to confirm
5. Save your backup codes in a secure location

**Supported Authenticator Apps:**
- Google Authenticator
- Microsoft Authenticator
- Authy
- 1Password
- LastPass Authenticator

**Using 2FA:**
1. Enter your email and password as usual
2. Open your authenticator app
3. Enter the 6-digit code for Kavach
4. Click "Verify" to complete login

### Account Activity Monitoring

**Viewing Login History:**
1. Go to Account Settings > Security
2. Click "Account Activity"
3. Review recent login attempts and locations

**What You'll See:**
- Login date and time
- IP address and approximate location
- Device and browser information
- Success/failure status

**Suspicious Activity:**
If you notice unfamiliar activity:
1. Change your password immediately
2. Enable 2FA if not already active
3. Log out all other sessions
4. Contact support

### Session Management

**Active Sessions:**
View and manage all active sessions:
1. Go to Account Settings > Security
2. Click "Active Sessions"
3. See all devices where you're logged in

**Ending Sessions:**
- Click "End Session" next to specific devices
- Or click "End All Other Sessions" for security
- You'll remain logged in on your current device

## Account Recovery

### If You Can't Access Your Email

1. **Contact Support Immediately**
   - Use the support contact form
   - Provide as much account information as possible
   - Include alternative contact methods

2. **Verification Process**
   - Support will verify your identity
   - You may need to provide additional information
   - Process may take 24-48 hours

### If Your Account is Compromised

**Immediate Steps:**
1. Change your password if you still have access
2. Enable 2FA immediately
3. End all other sessions
4. Review and update recovery information

**If Locked Out:**
1. Contact support immediately
2. Report the suspected compromise
3. Provide details about suspicious activity
4. Follow support's recovery instructions

## Security Best Practices

### Password Security
- Use a unique password for Kavach
- Enable 2FA for maximum security
- Don't share your login credentials
- Log out on shared computers

### Account Monitoring
- Regularly review account activity
- Set up email notifications for logins
- Monitor for unfamiliar devices or locations
- Report suspicious activity immediately

### Safe Login Practices
- Always log in through the official website
- Look for the secure lock icon (HTTPS)
- Don't click login links in emails
- Use bookmarks for quick access

## Troubleshooting Common Issues

### Can't Remember Email Address
- Check your email accounts for Kavach messages
- Try common email variations you use
- Contact support with any identifying information

### Browser Issues
- Clear your browser cache and cookies
- Try an incognito/private browsing window
- Update your browser to the latest version
- Try a different browser

### Mobile App Issues
- Update the app to the latest version
- Restart the app completely
- Check your internet connection
- Try logging in through the web browser

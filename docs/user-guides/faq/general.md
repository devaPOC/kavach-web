# General FAQ

This section answers the most frequently asked questions about the Kavach platform. If you can't find your answer here, check our [troubleshooting guide](troubleshooting.md) or contact support.

## Account and Registration

### How do I create an account?

1. Visit the Kavach homepage
2. Click "Sign Up" in the top right corner
3. Choose your account type (Customer or Expert)
4. Fill out the registration form with your information
5. Verify your email address by clicking the link sent to your inbox
6. Complete your profile setup

For detailed instructions, see our [account creation guide](../getting-started/account-creation.md).

### What's the difference between Customer and Expert accounts?

**Customer Accounts:**
- For users who want to access services on the platform
- Simplified registration process
- Access to browse and hire experts
- Customer-focused dashboard and tools

**Expert Accounts:**
- For service providers and professionals
- Additional verification requirements
- Ability to offer services and receive payments
- Expert-focused tools and analytics

### Can I change my account type after registration?

Currently, you cannot change your account type directly. However, you can:
- Create a second account with the different type
- Contact support to discuss account linking options
- Maintain separate accounts for different purposes

### Is there a cost to create an account?

No, creating an account on Kavach is completely free for both customers and experts. We only charge fees when transactions occur on the platform.

### How do I verify my email address?

After registration, you'll receive an email with the subject "Verify Your Kavach Account":
1. Check your inbox (and spam folder)
2. Click the "Verify Email Address" button in the email
3. You'll be redirected to a confirmation page
4. Your account is now verified and ready to use

If you don't receive the email within 10 minutes, click "Resend Verification Email" on the login page.

## Profile and Personal Information

### How do I update my profile information?

1. Click your profile picture/name in the top navigation
2. Select "Profile" from the dropdown menu
3. Click "Edit Profile" button
4. Make your changes
5. Click "Save Changes" to update

### Can I delete my account?

Yes, you can delete your account by:
1. Going to Account Settings > Privacy & Security
2. Scrolling to the bottom of the page
3. Clicking "Delete Account"
4. Following the confirmation process

**Note:** Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be removed from our systems.

### How do I change my password?

1. Go to Account Settings > Security
2. Find the "Change Password" section
3. Enter your current password
4. Enter your new password
5. Confirm your new password
6. Click "Update Password"

You'll receive an email confirmation, and all other sessions will be logged out.

### What should I do if I forgot my password?

1. Go to the login page
2. Click "Forgot Password?"
3. Enter your email address
4. Check your email for reset instructions
5. Click the reset link (valid for 1 hour)
6. Create a new password
7. Log in with your new password

## Platform Usage

### How do I search for experts?

1. Use the search bar on the homepage or navigation
2. Enter keywords related to your needs
3. Apply filters to narrow results:
   - Service category
   - Location
   - Price range
   - Ratings
   - Availability
4. Browse expert profiles and compare options

### How do I contact an expert?

1. Visit the expert's profile page
2. Click "Contact" or "Send Message"
3. Write your inquiry with project details
4. Click "Send Message"
5. The expert will receive your message and can respond directly

### What information should I include when contacting an expert?

Include these details in your initial message:
- Brief description of your project or needs
- Timeline and deadlines
- Budget range (if comfortable sharing)
- Any specific requirements or preferences
- Questions about their experience or approach

### How do I know if an expert is qualified?

Look for these indicators of qualification:
- **Verification Badge**: Platform-verified credentials
- **Reviews and Ratings**: Feedback from previous clients
- **Portfolio Samples**: Examples of their work
- **Professional Background**: Education, experience, certifications
- **Response Quality**: How they communicate and understand your needs

### What if I'm not satisfied with an expert's work?

1. **Communicate First**: Discuss your concerns directly with the expert
2. **Request Revisions**: Most experts will make reasonable adjustments
3. **Document Issues**: Keep records of all communications
4. **Platform Support**: Contact our support team if direct resolution fails
5. **Dispute Resolution**: Use our formal dispute process if necessary

## Payments and Billing

### How does payment work on the platform?

**For Customers:**
- Add payment methods to your account
- Payments are processed securely through the platform
- You're protected by our payment guarantee
- Receive invoices and receipts for all transactions

**For Experts:**
- Receive payments through the platform
- Funds are held securely until project completion
- Platform fees are deducted automatically
- Payments are transferred to your bank account

### What payment methods are accepted?

We accept:
- Major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express)
- Debit cards
- Bank transfers (in supported regions)
- Digital wallets (PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay)

### Are there any fees?

**For Customers:**
- No fees for browsing or contacting experts
- Small processing fee may apply to payments (typically 2-3%)

**For Experts:**
- Platform commission on completed projects (typically 10-15%)
- No fees for profile creation or maintenance
- Payment processing fees may apply

### When are payments processed?

- **Project Start**: Initial payment or deposit is held securely
- **Milestones**: Payments released as agreed milestones are completed
- **Project Completion**: Final payment released after customer approval
- **Disputes**: Payments held until resolution

### How do refunds work?

Refunds are handled case-by-case based on:
- Project terms and agreements
- Work completed and delivered
- Platform policies and guidelines
- Dispute resolution outcomes

Contact support to discuss refund requests.

## Security and Privacy

### Is my personal information secure?

Yes, we take security seriously:
- All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
- We follow industry-standard security practices
- Regular security audits and updates
- Strict access controls for our team
- Compliance with privacy regulations

### Who can see my profile information?

This depends on your privacy settings:
- **Public Information**: Name, profile picture, professional summary
- **Contact Information**: Only shared based on your preferences
- **Private Information**: Email, phone, payment details remain private
- **Activity**: You control what activity information is visible

### How do I report suspicious activity?

If you notice suspicious activity:
1. **Immediate Action**: Change your password and log out other sessions
2. **Report**: Use the "Report" button on profiles or messages
3. **Contact Support**: Email security@kavach.com with details
4. **Document**: Keep screenshots and records of suspicious behavior

### Can I block or report users?

Yes, you can:
- **Block Users**: Prevent them from contacting you
- **Report Profiles**: Flag inappropriate or suspicious accounts
- **Report Messages**: Flag spam or inappropriate communications
- **Report Projects**: Flag problematic project behavior

## Technical Issues

### The website isn't loading properly. What should I do?

Try these troubleshooting steps:
1. **Refresh the page** (Ctrl+F5 or Cmd+Shift+R)
2. **Clear browser cache and cookies**
3. **Try a different browser** (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
4. **Check your internet connection**
5. **Disable browser extensions** temporarily
6. **Contact support** if issues persist

### I can't upload files or images. What's wrong?

Common solutions:
1. **Check file size**: Maximum 10MB for most uploads
2. **Check file format**: JPG, PNG, GIF for images; PDF, DOC for documents
3. **Try a different browser**
4. **Clear browser cache**
5. **Check internet connection stability**

### The mobile app isn't working correctly.

For mobile app issues:
1. **Update the app** to the latest version
2. **Restart the app** completely
3. **Restart your device**
4. **Check for device OS updates**
5. **Reinstall the app** if problems persist
6. **Contact support** with device and app version details

### I'm not receiving email notifications.

Check these common causes:
1. **Spam/Junk Folder**: Check if emails are being filtered
2. **Email Settings**: Verify notification preferences in your account
3. **Email Address**: Ensure your email address is correct and verified
4. **Email Provider**: Some providers block automated emails
5. **Whitelist**: Add our email addresses to your contacts

## Platform Policies

### What are the community guidelines?

Our community guidelines require:
- **Respectful Communication**: Professional and courteous interactions
- **Honest Representation**: Accurate profiles and service descriptions
- **Quality Work**: Deliver services as promised
- **Fair Pricing**: Reasonable and transparent pricing
- **No Discrimination**: Equal treatment regardless of background
- **Legal Compliance**: All activities must be legal and ethical

### What happens if I violate platform policies?

Policy violations may result in:
- **Warning**: First-time minor violations
- **Temporary Suspension**: Repeated or moderate violations
- **Account Restriction**: Limited platform access
- **Permanent Ban**: Serious or repeated violations
- **Legal Action**: For illegal activities or fraud

### Can I use the platform for any type of service?

We allow most professional services, but prohibit:
- Illegal activities or services
- Adult or inappropriate content
- Gambling or betting services
- Multi-level marketing schemes
- Services that violate intellectual property
- Dangerous or harmful activities

### How do I appeal a platform decision?

If you disagree with a platform decision:
1. **Contact Support**: Explain your situation clearly
2. **Provide Evidence**: Include relevant documentation
3. **Appeal Process**: Follow our formal appeal procedures
4. **Review**: Our team will review your case
5. **Decision**: You'll receive a final decision within 5-7 business days

You can reach our support team through:

- **Support Tickets**: Submit detailed requests through our support system

Our typical response times:

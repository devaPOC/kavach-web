# User Troubleshooting Guide

This guide helps you resolve common issues you might encounter while using the Kavach platform. Issues are organized by category for easy navigation.

## Account and Login Issues

### Can't Log In to My Account

**Symptoms:**
- "Invalid credentials" error message
- Login page keeps refreshing
- Account appears to be locked

**Solutions:**

1. **Check Your Credentials**
   - Verify you're using the correct email address
   - Ensure your password is entered correctly (check caps lock)
   - Try typing your password in a text editor first to verify

2. **Password Reset**
   - Click "Forgot Password?" on the login page
   - Enter your email address
   - Check your email (including spam folder) for reset instructions
   - Follow the link to create a new password

3. **Account Lockout**
   - Wait 15 minutes if you've had multiple failed attempts
   - Clear your browser cache and cookies
   - Try logging in from a different browser or device

4. **Email Verification**
   - Check if your account email is verified
   - Look for verification emails in your inbox
   - Click "Resend Verification" if needed

**Still Having Issues?**
- Contact support with your account email
- Try accessing from a different network
- Check if your account has been suspended

### Email Verification Problems

**Symptoms:**
- Not receiving verification emails
- Verification link doesn't work
- "Email already verified" but still can't access features

**Solutions:**

1. **Check Email Delivery**
   - Look in spam/junk folders
   - Check if emails are being blocked by your provider
   - Add noreply@kavach.com to your contacts
   - Try a different email address if possible

2. **Resend Verification**
   - Go to the login page
   - Click "Resend Verification Email"
   - Wait 5-10 minutes for delivery
   - Check all email folders

3. **Link Issues**
   - Ensure you're clicking the link from the correct email
   - Check if the link has expired (valid for 24 hours)
   - Copy and paste the link into your browser if clicking doesn't work
   - Try opening the link in an incognito/private browser window

4. **Browser Issues**
   - Clear browser cache and cookies
   - Disable browser extensions temporarily
   - Try a different browser

**Advanced Solutions:**
- Contact your email provider about blocked emails
- Try verification from a different device
- Contact support to manually verify your account

### Profile and Settings Issues

**Symptoms:**
- Can't update profile information
- Changes aren't being saved
- Profile appears incomplete despite filling all fields

**Solutions:**

1. **Form Validation Issues**
   - Check for red error messages near form fields
   - Ensure all required fields are completed
   - Verify email format is correct
   - Check password meets requirements

2. **File Upload Problems**
   - Verify file size is under 5MB for images
   - Use supported formats (JPG, PNG, GIF for images)
   - Try compressing large images
   - Clear browser cache before uploading

3. **Browser Compatibility**
   - Update your browser to the latest version
   - Try a different browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
   - Disable ad blockers and extensions
   - Enable JavaScript and cookies

4. **Network Issues**
   - Check your internet connection stability
   - Try uploading from a different network
   - Wait and try again if the server is busy

**Data Not Saving:**
- Don't navigate away before clicking "Save"
- Check for error messages or notifications
- Try saving sections individually
- Contact support if data keeps disappearing

## Communication and Messaging Issues

### Messages Not Sending or Receiving

**Symptoms:**
- Messages appear to send but aren't received
- Not getting notifications for new messages
- Message history is missing

**Solutions:**

1. **Check Message Status**
   - Look for delivery confirmations (checkmarks)
   - Verify the recipient's username is correct
   - Check if messages are in your "Sent" folder

2. **Notification Settings**
   - Go to Account Settings > Notifications
   - Ensure message notifications are enabled
   - Check both email and in-app notification settings
   - Verify your email address is correct

3. **Browser and App Issues**
   - Refresh the page or restart the app
   - Clear browser cache and cookies
   - Update the mobile app to the latest version
   - Try accessing from a different device

4. **Network Connectivity**
   - Check your internet connection
   - Try switching between WiFi and mobile data
   - Disable VPN if you're using one

**Message History Missing:**
- Check if you accidentally deleted conversations
- Look in archived or spam message folders
- Contact support to recover important messages

### Expert Not Responding

**Symptoms:**
- Sent messages to expert but no response
- Expert appears active but doesn't reply
- Automatic responses only

**Solutions:**

1. **Check Response Expectations**
   - Review the expert's profile for typical response times
   - Allow 24-48 hours for initial responses
   - Consider time zone differences

2. **Message Quality**
   - Ensure your message is clear and specific
   - Include relevant project details
   - Ask specific questions
   - Be professional and courteous

3. **Expert Availability**
   - Check if the expert's status shows as "Available"
   - Look for vacation or busy indicators
   - Try contacting during their stated working hours

4. **Follow-Up Appropriately**
   - Send one polite follow-up after 48 hours
   - Don't send multiple messages in quick succession
   - Consider contacting other experts as backup

**Escalation Steps:**
- Report unresponsive experts after 5 business days
- Contact platform support for assistance
- Look for alternative experts with better response rates

## Payment and Billing Issues

### Payment Method Problems

**Symptoms:**
- Credit card is declined
- Payment fails during checkout
- Can't add new payment methods

**Solutions:**

1. **Card Verification**
   - Check card number, expiry date, and CVV
   - Ensure billing address matches card details
   - Verify card has sufficient funds/credit limit
   - Contact your bank about international transactions

2. **Payment Method Issues**
   - Try a different credit/debit card
   - Use PayPal or other supported payment methods
   - Check if your card supports online transactions
   - Ensure your card isn't expired

3. **Browser and Security**
   - Disable ad blockers during payment
   - Ensure JavaScript and cookies are enabled
   - Try an incognito/private browsing window
   - Clear browser cache and cookies

4. **Bank and Security Blocks**
   - Contact your bank about the declined transaction
   - Inform your bank about the Kavach platform
   - Check for fraud alerts or security holds
   - Try the payment from a different location/device

**Recurring Payment Issues:**
- Update expired card information
- Check for sufficient funds before payment dates
- Contact support to update payment schedules

### Billing Disputes and Refunds

**Symptoms:**
- Charged for services not received
- Incorrect amount charged
- Need to dispute a transaction

**Solutions:**

1. **Review Transaction Details**
   - Check your account's billing history
   - Review the original project agreement
   - Look for any additional fees or charges
   - Compare with email confirmations

2. **Contact the Expert First**
   - Discuss the issue directly with the service provider
   - Provide specific details about your concerns
   - Request clarification on charges
   - Try to resolve amicably

3. **Platform Dispute Process**
   - Use the built-in dispute resolution system
   - Provide documentation of the issue
   - Include screenshots and communication records
   - Follow the guided dispute process

4. **Refund Requests**
   - Contact support with refund justification
   - Provide evidence of service issues
   - Include relevant communication history
   - Allow 5-7 business days for review

**Prevention Tips:**
- Always clarify pricing before starting projects
- Keep records of all agreements and communications
- Use the platform's payment protection features
- Read terms and conditions carefully

## Technical and Performance Issues

### Website Loading Problems

**Symptoms:**
- Pages load slowly or not at all
- Images and content don't display properly
- Frequent timeouts or error messages

**Solutions:**

1. **Browser Troubleshooting**
   - Hard refresh the page (Ctrl+F5 or Cmd+Shift+R)
   - Clear browser cache and cookies
   - Disable browser extensions and ad blockers
   - Try an incognito/private browsing window

2. **Network Issues**
   - Check your internet connection speed
   - Try a different network (mobile data vs WiFi)
   - Restart your router/modem
   - Contact your internet service provider

3. **Browser Compatibility**
   - Update your browser to the latest version
   - Try a different browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
   - Enable JavaScript and cookies
   - Check browser security settings

4. **Device Issues**
   - Restart your computer or mobile device
   - Close other applications using internet
   - Check available storage space
   - Update your operating system

**Server-Side Issues:**
- Check our status page for known outages
- Try accessing the site later
- Contact support to report persistent issues

### Mobile App Problems

**Symptoms:**
- App crashes or freezes frequently
- Features don't work properly
- Can't log in through the app

**Solutions:**

1. **App Maintenance**
   - Update the app to the latest version
   - Restart the app completely (force close and reopen)
   - Restart your mobile device
   - Check for device OS updates

2. **Storage and Performance**
   - Free up storage space on your device
   - Close other apps running in the background
   - Check if your device meets minimum requirements
   - Clear the app's cache (Android) or reinstall (iOS)

3. **Network and Permissions**
   - Check your mobile data or WiFi connection
   - Ensure the app has necessary permissions
   - Try switching between WiFi and mobile data
   - Check if mobile data is enabled for the app

4. **Account Sync Issues**
   - Log out and log back in
   - Check if your account works on the website
   - Verify your account is in good standing
   - Contact support for account-specific issues

**Reinstallation:**
- Delete the app completely
- Restart your device
- Download and install the latest version
- Log in with your credentials

### File Upload and Download Issues

**Symptoms:**
- Can't upload profile pictures or documents
- Downloads fail or are corrupted
- Upload progress stalls or fails

**Solutions:**

1. **File Requirements**
   - Check file size limits (typically 5-10MB)
   - Verify supported file formats
   - Ensure file isn't corrupted
   - Try uploading a different file first

2. **Browser and Connection**
   - Use a stable internet connection
   - Try a different browser
   - Disable browser extensions
   - Clear browser cache and cookies

3. **File Preparation**
   - Compress large files before uploading
   - Rename files to remove special characters
   - Convert to supported formats if necessary
   - Scan files for viruses/malware

4. **Alternative Methods**
   - Try uploading from a different device
   - Use a different network connection
   - Upload during off-peak hours
   - Contact support for large file assistance

## Search and Discovery Issues

### Can't Find Experts or Services

**Symptoms:**
- Search returns no results
- Relevant experts don't appear in searches
- Filters don't work properly

**Solutions:**

1. **Search Optimization**
   - Use broader, more general keywords
   - Try different search terms and synonyms
   - Remove some filters to expand results
   - Check spelling and try alternative spellings

2. **Filter Settings**
   - Clear all filters and search again
   - Apply filters one at a time
   - Check location settings and expand radius
   - Verify price range isn't too restrictive

3. **Browse Categories**
   - Use category browsing instead of search
   - Explore related service categories
   - Look at featured or recommended experts
   - Check recently active experts

4. **Search Tips**
   - Use specific but not overly narrow terms
   - Include location if geography matters
   - Try searching by expertise rather than service names
   - Look at similar successful searches

**No Results Found:**
- Expand your search criteria
- Try searching in related categories
- Consider remote/online service providers
- Contact support for assistance finding experts

### Expert Profiles Not Loading

**Symptoms:**
- Expert profile pages are blank or incomplete
- Images and portfolio items don't display
- Contact buttons don't work

**Solutions:**

1. **Browser Issues**
   - Refresh the page
   - Clear browser cache and cookies
   - Try a different browser
   - Disable ad blockers and extensions

2. **Network Problems**
   - Check your internet connection
   - Try a different network
   - Wait and try again later
   - Use mobile data instead of WiFi

3. **Profile-Specific Issues**
   - Try viewing other expert profiles
   - Check if the expert's account is active
   - Look for profile update notifications
   - Contact the expert directly if possible

**Reporting Issues:**
- Report broken profiles to support
- Include the expert's name and profile URL
- Describe what's not working
- Try accessing from different devices

## Getting Additional Help

### When to Contact Support

Contact our support team when:
- You've tried the troubleshooting steps above
- The issue affects your ability to use core platform features
- You suspect a security or privacy issue
- You need help with account recovery
- You're experiencing billing or payment problems

### How to Contact Support

**Preparation:**
- Gather relevant information (account email, error messages, screenshots)
- Note what troubleshooting steps you've already tried
- Have your account information ready
- Document the issue clearly

**Contact Methods:**
- **In-Platform Help**: Use the help button in your dashboard
- **Support Tickets**: Submit detailed requests through our support system
- **Live Chat**: Available during business hours for urgent issues
- **Email**: support@kavach.com for general inquiries

### Information to Include

When contacting support, include:
- **Account Details**: Email address associated with your account
- **Issue Description**: Clear explanation of what's happening
- **Error Messages**: Exact text of any error messages
- **Screenshots**: Visual evidence of the problem
- **Browser/Device**: What you're using to access the platform
- **Steps Taken**: What troubleshooting you've already tried
- **Urgency**: How critical the issue is to your work

### Response Times

Typical support response times:
- **Critical Issues**: Within 4 hours
- **General Inquiries**: Within 24 hours
- **Complex Technical Issues**: 2-3 business days
- **Account Recovery**: 24-48 hours

### Self-Help Resources

Before contacting support, check:
- **FAQ Section**: Common questions and answers
- **User Guides**: Step-by-step tutorials
- **Community Forums**: Peer support and discussions
- **Status Page**: Known issues and maintenance updates
- **Video Tutorials**: Visual guides for complex processes

### Emergency Situations

For urgent security issues:
- **Suspected Account Compromise**: Change password immediately and contact security@kavach.com
- **Payment Fraud**: Contact both support and your bank/credit card company
- **Harassment or Abuse**: Use the report function and contact support immediately
- **Platform Outages**: Check our status page and social media for updates

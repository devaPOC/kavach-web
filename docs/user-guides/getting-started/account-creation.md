# Account Creation Guide

This guide walks you through creating your Kavach account step by step.

## Before You Start

### Requirements
- A valid email address
- A secure password (see [password requirements](#password-requirements))
- Access to your email for verification

### Supported Browsers
- Chrome 90+
- Firefox 88+
- Safari 14+
- Edge 90+

## Step-by-Step Account Creation

### Step 1: Access the Registration Page

1. Navigate to the Kavach platform
2. Click the **"Sign Up"** button in the top right corner
3. You'll be taken to the registration page

### Step 2: Choose Your Account Type

Select the type of account that best describes your role:

#### Customer Account
- For users who want to access services on the platform
- Simplified registration process
- Access to customer features and tools

#### Expert Account
- For service providers and professionals
- Additional profile fields for qualifications
- Access to expert tools and features

### Step 3: Fill Out Basic Information

Complete the registration form with the following information:

#### Required Fields
- **Email Address**: Must be a valid, accessible email
- **Password**: Must meet security requirements (see below)
- **Confirm Password**: Must match your password exactly
- **First Name**: Your given name
- **Last Name**: Your family name

#### Optional Fields (Customer)
- **Phone Number**: For account recovery and notifications
- **Date of Birth**: For age verification if required

#### Additional Fields (Expert)
- **Professional Title**: Your job title or profession
- **Company/Organization**: Where you work (if applicable)
- **Years of Experience**: Your professional experience level

### Step 4: Review Terms and Privacy Policy

1. Read the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy
2. Check the boxes to accept both documents
3. Optionally, subscribe to platform updates and newsletters

### Step 5: Complete Registration

1. Click the **"Create Account"** button
2. You'll see a confirmation message
3. Check your email for a verification message

## Email Verification

### What to Expect
After registration, you'll receive an email with the subject "Verify Your Kavach Account"

### Verification Steps
1. Open the verification email
2. Click the **"Verify Email Address"** button
3. You'll be redirected to a confirmation page
4. Your account is now verified and ready to use

### Didn't Receive the Email?
If you don't receive the verification email within 5 minutes:

1. Check your spam/junk folder
2. Ensure you entered the correct email address
3. Click **"Resend Verification Email"** on the login page
4. Contact support if you still don't receive it

## Password Requirements

Your password must meet the following criteria:

### Minimum Requirements
- At least 8 characters long
- Contains at least one uppercase letter (A-Z)
- Contains at least one lowercase letter (a-z)
- Contains at least one number (0-9)
- Contains at least one special character (!@#$%^&*)

### Password Strength Tips
- Use a unique password not used elsewhere
- Consider using a passphrase with multiple words
- Avoid personal information like names or birthdays
- Use a password manager for best security

### Example Strong Passwords
- `MySecure2024!Pass`
- `Coffee&Code123`
- `BlueSky$Morning9`

## Common Registration Issues

### Email Already Exists
If you see "Email already registered":
1. Try logging in instead of registering
2. Use the "Forgot Password" feature if needed
3. Contact support if you believe this is an error

### Password Too Weak
If your password is rejected:
1. Review the password requirements above
2. Add more complexity (numbers, symbols, mixed case)
3. Make it longer (12+ characters recommended)

### Verification Email Issues
If verification fails:
1. Ensure you're clicking the link from the correct email
2. Check if the link has expired (valid for 24 hours)
3. Request a new verification email

## After Account Creation

Once your account is verified:

1. **Complete Your Profile**: Add additional information to enhance your experience
2. **Set Up Security**: Enable two-factor authentication for added security
3. **Explore Features**: Familiarize yourself with the platform's capabilities
